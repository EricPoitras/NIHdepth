Instructions

Role-play a person you might work with in your practice.

Groups of 3 or 4: Participant, Clinician, and 1 or 2 Observers.

Identify a clear behavioral change goal. You are ambivalent and may not want to make a change now. You know you should change or want to change, but haven't made a change yet.

Scenario

CLINICIAN ROLE:

Explain why the participant should make this change.

  • Explain why the participant should make this change.
  • Give at least 3 specific benefits that would result if they made this change.
  • Tell the participant how they could make the change.
  • Emphasize how important it is to make the change.
  • Persuade the participant to make this change. If the participant still does not want to change, repeat the above!
OBSERVER ROLE:

Pay attention to the feelings expressed by both Participant and Clinician. Make note of them. Offer observations.
