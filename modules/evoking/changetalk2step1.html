Thank you for submitting your responses, please select the Next step to review all the client statements corresponding to change talk.

Counselor: May I ask you, could we spend a few minutes talking about alcohol, how it helps and what else you’ve noticed about it?

Client: Well as I said, it calms my nerves, but I can’t go on like this forever

Counselor: Although it helps, you’re concerned about it

Client: Well I’m not an alcoholic you know but I can’t be drinking with the kids around

Counselor: Because you want to be a responsible mother

Client: Yes, they are my No 1. priority

Counselor: And if you drink you worry you might neglect them

Client: No, never, I won’t neglect them, but I get like selfish and shout at them

Counselor: And that’s not fair to them

Client: Exactly, but then I get trapped when we leave for school, I either calm my nerves with a drink or shake like a baby, and that’s not good for them either to see me like that

Counselor: like you are stuck in a corner, with no way out,

Client: Well that’s why I’m here I guess, to find a way out

Counselor: Well what will be the most helpful for you now?

Client: To see if there is a way to get out of the house without drinking

Counselor: And to get more control over those panicky feelings

Client: Yes, that’s me. I’ve got to do it differently now.
